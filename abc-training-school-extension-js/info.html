Welcome to ABC Training School Copilot Extension

This extension provides AI-powered support for athletes, coaches, tournament organizers, sponsors, and alumni.

  • Coaching: Expert guidance from experienced coaches.
  • Tournaments: Information and support for local and national events.
  • Sponsors: Connect with sponsors and resources.
  • Alumni: Learn about our award-winning alumni.
  • Tournament Scheduler: View and register for upcoming tournaments.
  • Coach Directory: Browse profiles of our expert coaches.

Use the chat feature to ask questions or get help related to ABC Training School.
